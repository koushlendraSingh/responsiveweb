The mars rover nasa

The University Rover Challenge (URC) by the Mars Society is a robotics competition for university level students that challenges teams to design and build a rover that would be of use to early explorers on Mars

Keeping up with their tradition since last two years Mars Rover Manipal was the first team to submit the SAR. MRM is one of the five teams from India to be selected, along with teams from IIT Bombay, VIT Vellore, SRM University, and IIT Madras. Mars Rover Manipal is very excited since the declaration of the results and is working even harder. The rover for URC 2018 is named ‘Airawat’. The rover is capable of performing tasks such as autonomous traversal, extreme retrieval, equipment servicing and soil collection and testing amongst others. A team of 20 members have travelled to USA to represent Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) on the big stage. Bridenstine’s career in federal service began in the U.S. Navy
